## Important Limitations

### Data
- **Synthetic Data**: All data is artificially generated and does not represent real agricultural conditions
- **Simplified Models**: Mathematical relationships are simplified and may not reflect complex agricultural systems
- **No Real-World Validation**: Models have not been validated against actual agricultural data

### Agricultural Accuracy
- **Oversimplified**: Real crop yield depends on numerous factors not included in this model
- **No Pest/Disease Modeling**: Critical agricultural factors like pests, diseases, and extreme weather are not considered
- **Limited Crop Types**: Model assumes generic crop behavior without species-specific characteristics
- **No Seasonal Dynamics**: Temporal variations and seasonal patterns are not modeled

### Technical Limitations
- **Spatial Resolution**: Geographic modeling is coarse and not suitable for field-level decisions
- **Feature Engineering**: Advanced agricultural features like soil composition, microclimate, and management practices are simplified
- **Model Assumptions**: Statistical assumptions may not hold for real agricultural systems
